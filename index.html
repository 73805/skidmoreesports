We play games

Video games are what we do, and we do them well. Whether you're looking for chances to compete on campus, rep Skidmore in collegiate tournaments, or just LTP (learn to play) we have you covered.

Leadership

Skidmore eSports is a student run, SGA approved club. The eBoard is structured to give representatives for popular games the support they need to lead their communities. Weekly eBoard meetings are used to delegate responsibilities, keep track of SGA funds, and plan events. Everything from casual smash nights to official jerseys starts with the eBoard. Representing Skidmore College

With just over 2,500 students, Skidmore College is a surprisingly strong contender in collegiate eSports. Each year the club organizes as many teams as needed to serve players at each skill level. Official tournament organizers like the Collegiate Star League and Tespa offer competition in some of the biggest games at the Division 1, 2 and 3 levels. Beyond the big tournaments, we leverage our friend lists to set inter-collegiate play with regional schools. As a club, we strive to organize a variety of ways for students to represent Skidmore College in collegiate eSports; for fun, bragging rights, and maybe even their resume.

Swag (Swag)

Great swag opportunities are an integral part of eSports tradition. In 2015 we worked with the equipment manager of Skidmore's athletic department to order dri-fit jerseys with the official College logo, and individualized (user)names for just $25 per player. Thanks to the design skills of club-forefather Raphael Galvis we also have our own sweet logo (Skidmore Thoroughbred Gaming) which looks great on mousepads and laptop stickers. Get in touch if you want to get some Skid eSports items and apparel, and let us know if you have more ideas!
